## Common artifact categories

- **Noise** (low dose / low signal)
- **Motion** (patient motion, cardiac/respiratory)
- **Metal** (beam hardening and streaks in CT)
- **Aliasing** (undersampling, especially in MRI)
- **Partial volume** (limited resolution mixing tissues)
